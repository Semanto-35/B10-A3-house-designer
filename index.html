FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

We'd love to hear from you. Whether you have questions,projects details or else fill free to ask.

What is the first step in starting a house design project?

The first step is to schedule an initial consultation with our team. During this meeting, we will discuss your ideas, needs, and budget. We’ll also review the site and any existing plans you may have.

How long does it take to complete a design project?

The duration of a design project varies depending on the complexity and scope. Simple renovations may take a few weeks, while new constructions or major remodels can take several months.

What factors affect the cost of a house design project?

Costs are influenced by factors such as the size of the project, the complexity of the design, materials used, and any special features or requirements. We offer a detailed estimate after discussing your project needs and preferences.

Do you offer design services for both new builds and renovations?

Yes, we offer design services for both new construction projects and home renovations. Whether you’re building from scratch or updating an existing space, we can help you achieve your vision.
